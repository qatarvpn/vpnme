How we handle your data

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Effective date:

1. Who we are

one vpn ("we", "our", or "us") provides a virtual private network (VPN) application designed to enhance your online privacy and security.

2. Information we collect

No activity logs. We do not monitor, log, or store your browsing activity, destination IPs, DNS queries, or the content of traffic tunneled through our VPN.

  • App diagnostics (optional): crash reports and performance metrics to improve reliability. These do not include your browsing content.
  • Advertising data: if ads are shown, advertising partners may receive device identifiers strictly to deliver and measure ads. Consent is requested where legally required.

3. Connection metadata

To operate the service, minimal technical data may be processed while a session is active, such as the server region, connection status, and total data transferred. This information is ephemeral and not associated with specific activity or destinations.

4. Cookies and local storage

The app itself does not use web cookies. Some third-party SDKs may use local storage or device identifiers for functionality such as advertising frequency capping or analytics, subject to your consent where applicable.

5. Advertising and consent

We may show ads using Google Mobile Ads. In regions where consent is required (e.g., the EU/EEA), we use Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP) to obtain and manage consent. You can change your preferences at any time via the app’s privacy options.

6. Third-party services

  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob): for displaying ads. See Google’s privacy policy for details on data processing.
  • Crash reporting/analytics (if enabled): used to improve app stability and performance; does not include browsing content.

7. Data security

We use industry-standard measures to protect information. While no system is perfectly secure, we continuously work to safeguard our infrastructure and your privacy.

8. Children’s privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

9. Your rights & choices

  • Consent management: where required, you can give or withdraw consent for advertising data processing.
  • Access, delete, or correct: contact us to exercise applicable rights under your local laws (e.g., GDPR/CCPA).

10. International transfers

Your data may be processed on servers located in different countries. We take steps to ensure appropriate safeguards are in place when required.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post any changes here and update the effective date above.
